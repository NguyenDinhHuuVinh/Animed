What is VTuber and why is it the future of the industry
VTubers are reshaping the field of online content creation by changing the look, voice, and actions of human YouTubers.
A VTuber (ブイチューバー, BuiChūbā), or virtual YouTuber (Japanese: バーチャルユーチューバー, Hepburn: bācharu YūChūbā), is an online entertainer who uses a virtual avatar generated using computer graphics. Real-time motion capture software or technology are often—but not always—used to capture movement.
